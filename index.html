Welcome, to this sample exercise. To begin, Enter Coordinates into the fields labelled Latitude and Longitude in the left panel. Then, Click 'Get Street View' to fetch the nearest Google Street View Panorama available on Google's servers. Next, Select a Filter under the 'Select Filter' category, to apply the desired effect on the current frame. Use Street View's embeded controls to Pan & Zoom to a desired frame of reference for generating statistical graphs. Finally, Select a Visualization under the 'Select Visualization' category to generate visualizations.

Note: Visualizations might take some time to process.
